BMI stands for Body Mass Index a simple calculation using your weight and height to estimate if you are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese. It's a quick way to understand your body's relative health status.

Why is BMI important?

Easy Health Indicator

Gives you an idea about your weight and its health challenges.

Tracks Progress

Helps you see changes as you lose fat or gain muscle.

Goal-Setting

Enables you in choosing the right workout and diet plan.

Motivation

Watching your BMI improve can boost motivation and keep you focused.
